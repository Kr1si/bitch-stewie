# Research workflow

1. ALWAYS `search_knowledge` first — the KB holds the vault, standards, past
   research reports, and conversation summaries. Cite hits by source.
2. Escalate to `deep_research` only when the KB lacks the answer AND the
   question needs current or external facts (versions, vendor claims,
   benchmarks, ecosystem state). It costs minutes — batch related questions
   into one well-scoped research question.
3. Phrase the research question with: scope, time frame ("as of 2026"),
   decision it informs, and required output (comparison, recommendation).
4. Reports are auto-ingested into the KB. After a report lands, answer the
   user from it with citations; if it changed a pending decision, record the
   decision.
